## Sprint Goal

The goal of Sprint 1 is to establish the initial planning and brainstorming of the project including user stories, product planning, product vision and environment setup. At sprint 1 we are preparing the project to enter the development phase in sprint 2.

The following is a part of product backlog. Full product backlog is accessbile through [this link](https://github.com/users/SeoBlack/projects/7/views/13).

A Sprint 1 task is considered done when:
